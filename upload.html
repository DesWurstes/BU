Here are some tips for uploading.
Tips
  • This website will compress your files for you, so please don't compress before uploading.
  • Uploading may take up to an hour depending on the file size.
  • If you want to have the verbose log of the process, you can use Inspect Element > Console.
  • In the rare case that there was an error, you'd see a red line. In that case, you should right click on the Console to save it to a file.
