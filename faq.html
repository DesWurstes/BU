Q: How does this work?

A: Creates fake addresses encoding data forming a part of multisig addresses, an address of which is real and used to redeem the funds.

Q: Does this harm the Bitcoin Cash network by bloating it?

A: Bitcoin is a decentralized network. If it were harmful, miners would not include it in their blocks. Nonetheless, uploaders, downloaders and every user is subject to laws and the terms of use.
